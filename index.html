Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2025

Farm Book is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Plot Name
  • Crop Name
  • Land Size
  • Optional user profile data (Name, Email)

2. Data Storage

All data entered into the Farm Book app is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store your data on any external servers unless explicitly stated during backup or sync operations.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data you provide is used only for personal record keeping and farm management. We do not share, sell, or use your information for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the safety of your data. Farm Book uses device-level security and relies on your device's default storage encryption. Please ensure your device is protected with a password or biometric lock.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View the data you’ve entered
  • Delete all stored data at any time via the app’s reset feature

6. Third-Party Services

Farm Book does not use third-party analytics, ads, or tracking tools. If future updates include external services, this policy will be updated accordingly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We encourage users to check this page periodically for changes. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
